我们是 NeuroGeneration 心理脑科学联盟（简称 NG），一个由热爱神经科学与心理学的中学生创立的非营利公益组织！ We are the NeuroGeneration Psychology and Neuroscience Alliance (NG), a non-profit organization founded by secondary school students passionate about neuroscience and psychology!

🧠 我们是谁？ 🧠 Who We Are

来自全国北京、江浙沪、大湾区等地区的中学生脑科学爱好者、BrainBee 全国脑科学竞赛获奖者 + 专业指导老师 + 学生社团联盟。

我们在此共同打造一个专注于心理健康与神经科学的交流平台！

A powerful alliance uniting high school neuroscience enthusiasts, BrainBee National Competition laureates, professional academic advisors, and student clubs across Beijing, the Yangtze River Delta, and the Greater Bay Area.

Together, we co-create an advanced platform dedicated to mental health and neuroscience interaction!

🎯 我们的目标？ 🎯 Our Mission

  • 搭建中学生神经科学与心理学的交流社群
  • 提供学术分享、社交互动与自我展示的机会
  • 用科学的方式关注心理健康，探索大脑奥秘
  • To build a collaborative community for high schoolers in neuroscience and psychology.
  • To provide opportunities for academic sharing, networking, and self-expression.
  • To raise mental health awareness through scientific approaches and explore the wonders of the human brain.
